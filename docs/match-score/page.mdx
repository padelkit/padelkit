# MatchScore

The `MatchScore` dataclass is an immutable snapshot of the match score at a specific point in time. It is dynamically generated by `MatchScoreHistory` and should never be modified directly. {{ className: 'lead' }}

## Properties

<Properties>
  <Property name="points_A" type="int | str">
    Current points of Team A (e.g., `0`, `15`, `30`, `40`, `"AD"`).
  </Property>
  <Property name="points_B" type="int | str">
    Current points of Team B.
  </Property>
  <Property name="games_A" type="int">
    Games won by Team A in the current set.
  </Property>
  <Property name="games_B" type="int">
    Games won by Team B in the current set.
  </Property>
  <Property name="sets_A" type="int">
    Total sets won by Team A.
  </Property>
  <Property name="sets_B" type="int">
    Total sets won by Team B.
  </Property>
  <Property name="completed_sets" type="list[tuple[int, int]]">
    A history of scores from completed sets, e.g., `[(6, 4), (7, 5)]`.
  </Property>
  <Property name="server" type="ServingState | None">
    <AddedIn version="2.0.1">
      The active serving team and player encapsulated in a [`ServingState`](/serving-state) object, or `None` if the match is completed.
    </AddedIn>
  </Property>
  <Property name="in_tiebreak" type="bool">
    <AddedIn version="2.0.1">
      Indicates whether a regular tie-break is currently active in the current set.
    </AddedIn>
  </Property>
  <Property name="in_super_tiebreak" type="bool">
    <AddedIn version="2.0.1">
      Indicates whether a match-deciding tie-break (or super tie-break) is currently active.
    </AddedIn>
  </Property>
  <Property name="is_gold_point" type="bool">
    <AddedIn version="2.0.1">
      Indicates whether the current game is at `40-40` and is being decided by a *Punto de Oro* (Golden Point) without advantages.
    </AddedIn>
  </Property>
</Properties>


## String Representation

When cast to a string via `print(score)` or `str(score)`, it provides a cleanly formatted tabular output, utilizing the teams' names if available:

```text
TEAM A (I. Swiatek / Otro Jugador) | 6 4 | 3 | 30
TEAM B (E. Svitolina / Otra)       | 4 6 | 5 | 40  *
```
*(The asterisk `*` indicates the team currently serving)*
